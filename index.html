Wombman Rising

The journey of the Afrikan Woman; capturing each and every moment of her ascendency. Still growing, learning and becoming. Wombman Rising is releasing the essence of unlimited purpose through spoken word, poetry and the power, sound and word of stories; the affirmation and authentic love of spreading knowledge.
