Wombman Rising

The journey of the Afrikan Woman; capturing each and every moment of her ascendency. Still growing, learning and becoming. Wombman Rising is releasing the essence of unlimited purpose through spoken word, poetry and the power, sound and word of stories; the affirmation and authentic love of spreading knowledge.

Haylee Venus

Poet / Writer

Two time GDST poetry competition winner.

She writes for the chance to be able to understand human emotion, since the the age of 16.

She says, “Poetry is genuinely the surprise I never expected. Poetry feels like a ‘tight hug’, and I can only hope that hug extends through my metaphors to reach you as well.”

She considers poetry to be her saving grace and wishes for others to find a little salvation in her words too.
